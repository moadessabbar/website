04.2019

Morphological analysis of Teddy Bears!

Among the great diversity of existing teddy bears, some are more comforting than others. Through this great experience, our research team wishes to determine, thanks to public participation, what are the morphological traits (eye size, hair length, etc.) that lead to this result.

The morphological analysis of the soft toys will be coupled with an analysis of the emotional and subjective relationship to the soft toy (aesthetic note, comfort index). Naturalistic and cross-cutting humanities approaches may provide answers to the question: "What are the characters that make a teddy bear comforting?". A classification tree of teddy bears analyzed in 12 French cities will make it possible to precisely determine which are the large “families” that make up the group of teddy bears.

The results of the experiment will shed light on the scientific study of an affective object on two axes (morphological and emotional), making it possible to classify specimens according to their ability to trigger emotions in children and adults. The statistical techniques used for these investigations are those used in the field of living studies (aesthetics of coral ecosystems for example) or cognitive psychology (evaluation of the attractiveness of faces under different experimental conditions, children's emotions following various stimuli, etc.).

Moulinex hand blender Cup Cover

Modular cover for Moulinex Hand Blender Cup. Prevents splashes while blending while keeping the blender mobile. The second piece completes the cover for full protection of the cup when the juice is to be preserved. 3D printable. Tested on Creality CR-10 and CR-X. Very little supports needed, a brim if necessary. The "Moulinex" logo is a separate file to make it printable in a different color, should be carefully placed using the slicing software.
